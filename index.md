# Welcome to ImageEval 2026

ImageEval 2026 is a shared task on culturally grounded Arabic multimodal AI. The task suite evaluates visual question answering, image-grounded hallucination detection, and cultural accuracy evaluation for text-to-image generation.

The shared task focuses on Arabic language varieties and culturally specific visual content from the MENA region, with dedicated tracks for participants to build, evaluate, and compare multimodal systems.
